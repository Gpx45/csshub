
3 Main ways of adding CSS to HTML documents:

-Inline styling
    PROS
        - Good for very specialized rules

    CONS
        - Time consuming to style multiple elements
        - Tricky to manage and update
        - Can get very confusing


-Embedded style sheets
    PROS
        -   Good for making a specific page that is very 
            different from the others

    CONS
        - Time consuming to apply global styles
        - Hard to maintain and update


-External style sheets
